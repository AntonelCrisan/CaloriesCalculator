Daily calorie calculator

It calculates the number of calories that must be consumed daily, it is useful for slimming, fattening and weight maintenance. With the help of this calculator, you can find out your caloric needs for 24 hours, and based on your body mass index, you will receive a message if you need to lose weight or gain weight. A calorie represents the amount of energy needed to raise the temperature of one kilogram of water by one degree Celsius. The nutrients from the food consumed are transformed into energy through the metabolic processes that take place in the human body, and the energy is consumed by maintaining the vital functions of the body, the basal metabolic rate and through the other activities carried out each person. Examples of vital functions that consume a large part of energy are: maintaining a constant temperature of ~37 degrees Celsius of the human body, heartbeat, digestion, functioning of all organs. The remaining energy is consumed through various activities that each individual carries out daily, through sports, work, thinking, stress and others. Each person's caloric consumption may vary depending on other factors such as environmental temperature, stress or metabolic problems, so the calculator should be used as a guide. It is important to select the level of physical activity you have daily.
Important: On most food products you will find the energy value expressed in kcalories, 1 kcal is equivalent to a large calorie used in nutrition.
1 calorie used in nutrition = 1 kcal = 4.187 kJ (kilojoules) The daily activities used in the calorie calculator are classified as follows:
  • sedentary - little or no exercise, office work;
  • average - light exercises / sports - physical work 1-3 days / week;
  • moderate - moderate exercises / sports - physical work 3-5 days / week;
  • intense - intense exercise / sports - physical work 6-7 days / week;
  • very intense - intense exercises every day / intense physical work or performance sports;
